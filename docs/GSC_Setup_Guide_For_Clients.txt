================================================================================
STEP 1: LOG INTO GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console

2. Select your GSC property (the one connected to your website)

   If your website is a domain (e.g., yoursite.com):
   - Select "Domain" and enter your domain (e.g., yoursite.com)
   - You may need to verify ownership via DNS, HTML file, or tag

   If your website is on a subdomain or path (e.g., shop.yoursite.com):
   - Select "URL prefix" and enter your full URL
   - Verify via one of the methods Google offers


================================================================================
STEP 2: GO TO SETTINGS
================================================================================

1. In the left sidebar, click the gear icon "Settings"


================================================================================
STEP 3: GO TO USERS AND PERMISSIONS
================================================================================

1. In the Settings page, click "Users and permissions"

2. You'll see a list of people who have access to this property


================================================================================
STEP 4: ADD KASPARRO'S SERVICE ACCOUNT
================================================================================

1. Click the "Add user" button

2. Enter this exact email address:

   kasparro-ga4-reader-kasparro-p@kasparro-audit-pillar-b.iam.gserviceaccount.com

3. Set Permission to "Viewer"
   (This is read-only. Kasparro cannot modify any data in your GSC.)

4. Click "Add" or "Invite"


================================================================================
STEP 5: FIND YOUR GSC PROPERTY URL
================================================================================

Kasparro needs your GSC property URL in the correct format.

1. Look at the URL of your property in GSC

2. The format is: sc-domain:yourdomain.com
   (e.g., sc-domain:skinq.com)

3. If your property is https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/owners?resource_id=sc-domain:yourdomain.com
   Then your GSC site URL is: sc-domain:yourdomain.com

4. Copy this format -- you need it when sharing with Kasparro


================================================================================
STEP 6: SHARE WITH KASPARRO
================================================================================

Send Kasparro the following:

  - GSC Property URL in format: sc-domain:yourdomain.com
    (e.g., sc-domain:skinq.com)

  IMPORTANT: Use "sc-domain:" prefix, NOT "https://"
  Wrong:  https://yourdomain.com
  Right:  sc-domain:yourdomain.com


================================================================================
WHAT HAPPENS NEXT
================================================================================

Once Kasparro receives your GSC property URL:

- Kasparro will use their service account to access your GSC data
- Kasparro will pull search clicks, impressions, CTR, and position data
- Kasparro will generate your brand audit report

No changes will be made to your GSC -- this is READ-ONLY access only.

You can remove access at any time by going to:
Search Console -> Settings -> Users and permissions -> Find Kasparro's email -> Remove

note:

1. If your website has multiple subdomains (e.g., shop.yoursite.com and
   blog.yoursite.com), each needs to be added separately in GSC.

2. Your GSC property must be verified before Kasparro can access it.
   If you haven't verified ownership yet, do that first.
